About

Created in Brasilia, Brazil, the Ocelot Games is a small independent game development studio founded in 2019 by a couple of friends who wanted to create fun games with immersive storytelling.

The Ocelot team currently have six members, and each of us have a specialized game development background, from renowned artists within Brasilia's Game Development Industry and scholars in Narrative Fiction to experienced Game Dev professionals. We unite artistic prowess, academic knowledge and practical experience to create new games and experiences.
